Jay Gatsby: A mysterious, fabulously wealthy millionaire driven by an obsessive,
 romantic obsession to win back his first love.
 Nick Carraway: The narrator 
 and Gatsby's neighbor. He observes the reckless behavior of the rich and 
 acts as the audience's window
 into the story.
 Daisy Buchanan: Nick's cousin, Gatsby’s former lover, and the glamorous but 
 somewhat fragile wife of Tom Buchanan.
 Tom Buchanan: Daisy's arrogant, affluent, 
 and unfaithful husband who comes from "old money".
 Jordan Baker: Daisy’s cynical,
  athletic friend and a professional golfer who becomes romantically involved 
  with 
  Nick.
  Myrtle Wilson: Tom’s vivacious secret mistress who lives in the 
  industrial "valley of ashes".George Wilson: Myrtle’s unsuspecting, 
  downtrodden husband who owns a local auto repair garage.
